About Me

Hey there! My name is Chance Eakin, and this is the beginning of my portfolio. A little bit about me and my former/current life:


I'm an opera singer! Or, at least, that's what I used to do! I hold degrees Baylor, the University of Cincinnati, and the University of Texas at Austin, so football season is...complicated. On the stage, I'm most at home with Mozart and Musical theater, and I maintain a love/hate relationship with the staging of Dante's works.


Aside from the singing, I'm a sixth generation Texan that loves BBQ and woodworking. When I'm not actively coding, I'm helping my folks remodel our farmhouse and perfecting how I smoke a brisket (hint: if you're using anything more than salt and pepper in your rub, you're doing it wrong).


I live with my awesome wife in North Austin, and I'm excited to learn more about coding, especially Node.js. Now, I could continue to talk about myself, or I could fill in this text with Meat Ipsum.
